Rewriting the Rules of Contract Review: How DocuSign’s AI Contract Analyzer Delivers Clarity, Speed, and Confidence

Contracts are the lifeblood of business — defining relationships, obligations, and value exchange. Yet for most organizations, the process of reviewing them remains slow, manual, and risk‑prone. The Modern Contract Review Challenge

Before agreements are signed, the contract review stage is often a bottleneck. Legal teams must sift through dense, complex language, identify deviations from standards, and align multiple versions between counterparties and internal stakeholders. This pain is compounded by:

  • Information overload: Lengthy contracts with nuanced, cross‑referenced clauses.
  • Inconsistent language: Differences in terms across agreements cause confusion and compliance gaps.
  • Time pressure: Contracts need to be turned quickly to avoid revenue or project delays.
  • Limited visibility: Stakeholders outside of legal struggle to interpret contractual risks.

Even with CLM tools, much of this work has remained human‑intensive — until now.

What Makes AI Contract Analyzer Different

Part of the DocuSign Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) portfolio announced at DocuSign Momentum24, AI Contract Analyzer is purpose‑built for pre‑execution contract intelligence. Unlike generic document search or summarization tools, it applies large language models trained on contracts to automatically:

  • Summarize agreements in plain language for quick comprehension.
  • Flag deviations from your standard terms, playbooks, or templates.
  • Compare multiple versions to pinpoint precisely what’s changed.
  • Extract critical clauses and data points for compliance and reporting.
  • Highlight risks specific to your business and regulatory environment.

The result: Teams can spend less time finding and interpreting differences, and more time making strategic decisions.
