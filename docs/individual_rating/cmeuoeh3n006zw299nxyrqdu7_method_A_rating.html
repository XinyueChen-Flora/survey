Quick Overview: Current AI Trends in Legal Tech

  • Embedded AI > standalone: Buyers want AI in their existing CLM/eSignature and productivity tools to minimize change management.
  • RAG on private data: Organizations prioritize models that ground on their clause libraries and templates; data isolation and residency matter.
  • Auditability and governance: Citations, confidence scoring, policy alignment, and admin guardrails are now table stakes.
  • Human-in-the-loop: AI accelerates review but legal teams require final control, traceability, and a full audit trail.
  • Regulation-ready: EU AI Act transparency and risk controls, GDPR, SOC 2/ISO 27001, and vendor data use disclosures are mandatory checkpoints.
  • Actionable insights: Shift from passive summaries to suggested clause rewrites, fallbacks, and automated tasks/tickets.
  • Consolidation and ROI: Preference for fewer vendors; measurable cycle-time reduction and compliance uplift expected in quarter(s), not years.
