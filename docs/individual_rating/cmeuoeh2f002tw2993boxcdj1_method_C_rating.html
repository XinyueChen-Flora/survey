Slack's New AI Meeting Summarizer Feature

Title: Introducing Slack’s AI Meeting Summarizer: Turn Every Meeting Into Outcomes

If your calendar is packed and your channels are busier than ever, you’re not alone. Hybrid work has expanded collaboration across time zones and teams—but it’s also expanded the number of meetings we’re all expected to join. Many of us are juggling live sessions, notes apps, shared docs, and follow-up messages just to keep momentum. It’s time for a simpler path from discussion to decision to done.

Today, we’re introducing Slack’s AI Meeting Summarizer to help every team turn meetings into outcomes—right where work happens. Instead of toggling between tools or digging through long transcripts, you’ll get focused summaries with decisions, owners, and next steps delivered to the right channel or canvas. Catch up in minutes, stay aligned asynchronously, and keep work moving with less overhead.

Why now: AI belongs in the flow of work

Over the past year, AI has moved from novelty to necessity inside the tools we use every day. Colleagues expect relevant answers in seconds, concise recaps after discussions, and help turning ideas into next steps. That shift is especially urgent for meetings. Not everyone can attend live; conversations are rich but fleeting; and the real value comes from what happens after the meeting ends.

Slack is where teams coordinate, align, and execute. With AI Meeting Summarizer, the outcomes of a discussion—decisions, owners, timelines—live alongside the work itself. That’s the difference between notes that get filed away and outcomes that drive momentum.

How Slack’s AI Meeting Summarizer works

  • Summarize across platforms: Whether you’re in a Slack huddle or using a supported external meeting tool, Slack can generate a meeting summary that captures the heart of the conversation.
  • Deliver recaps where they belong: Summaries post to the appropriate channel or canvas, so the right people can review, discuss, and keep things moving.
  • Turn talk into tasks: Decisions and action items are highlighted with owners and due dates. Teams can track them via Slack lists/workflows and get reminders automatically.
  • Keep knowledge discoverable: Summaries become part of your channel’s history. Use Slack AI to search for decisions, action items, and context later—no need to replay or rehash.

Meet less. Catch up faster. Execute more.

When outcomes are accessible in Slack, you don’t need everyone in the room to make progress. Smaller, focused meetings become the norm. Teammates who couldn’t attend can catch up in minutes with the recap and ask clarifying questions right in the channel. That means fewer status meetings, faster alignment, and more time for deep work.

Built for enterprise trust

Deploying AI at scale requires more than a clever feature. It requires trust. Slack’s AI Meeting Summarizer is built with enterprise-grade security and admin transparency in mind, aligning with the controls and governance options organizations rely on. Admins can configure access, retention, and sharing to match company policies. The result: Teams move faster without compromising on the standards that keep their data safe.

Designed to work across your tools

Your meetings don’t all happen in one place—and that’s okay. Slack’s AI Meeting Summarizer is designed to work across huddles and supported external meeting platforms. No matter where the conversation happens, the outcomes land in Slack, where cross-functional work continues.

How teams are using it today

  • Sales and Customer Success: After a customer call, the summary posts to the account channel with next steps and owners. Hand off seamlessly to solutions or support, and keep stakeholders in the loop via Slack Connect.
  • Product and Engineering: Sprint reviews and incident calls automatically produce structured outcomes with timelines and DRI assignments—easy to reference in future retros.
  • Marketing and Comms: Campaign reviews result in clear approvals, feedback, and deadlines, posted in the campaign channel so global teams can move forward asynchronously.
  • Support and Operations: Postmortems produce consistent, searchable summaries and action lists that improve future response and reliability.
  • HR and Recruiting: Interview debriefs generate standardized recaps and action items for hiring teams to review and track.

How we compare

Standalone note-taking tools have improved transcription and summaries, but they often leave actions stranded outside the team’s daily workflow. Platform-specific AI can deliver strong recaps inside a single meeting tool, yet outcomes can get siloed if your teams collaborate primarily in Slack. Slack’s AI Meeting Summarizer brings the best of both worlds: flexibility across meeting platforms with outcomes that live where your teams plan, discuss, and execute.

A smarter way to work, together

Meetings aren’t going away—but with the right AI in the right place, they can become lighter, clearer, and more effective. Slack’s AI Meeting Summarizer helps you focus on what matters: decisions made, actions owned, and progress measured. So you can meet less, catch up faster, and keep work moving—together.
