A day‑in‑the‑life: from chaos to clarity

A product marketing lead needs a campaign operations hub—today. She opens a Notion page and describes the workflow: channels, assets, owners, due dates, dependencies, and approvals. ADA proposes a database with the right properties, links it to the existing content library, and creates views for each role: PMM, design, and performance marketing.

Next, she imports a spreadsheet of active campaigns. ADA auto‑categorizes entries by channel, extracts asset types from descriptions, flags duplicates, and normalizes statuses. She asks for “all campaigns with assets overdue by more than 7 days grouped by owner.” ADA creates the view and a dashboard tile.

Finally, she sets a policy: if an asset is Blocked for 3 days, notify the owner and escalate to the channel lead. ADA drafts the automation. What used to take a week of building and cleanup now takes an afternoon—inside the same workspace as the team’s briefs, brand guidelines, and status notes.
