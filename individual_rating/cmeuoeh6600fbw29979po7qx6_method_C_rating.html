  • Lifestyle pillars as first-line ‚Äútherapies‚Äù: Multicomponent physical activity, dietary patterns rich in minimally processed plant foods and healthy fats, adequate sleep, smoking cessation, low-risk alcohol use (ideally avoidance), social connection, and stress management are supported by robust trials and meta-analyses.
